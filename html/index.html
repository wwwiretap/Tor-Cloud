Tor-Cloud EC2 - Easy, Flexible & Awesome!

Amazon Web Services (AWS) is the provider of the cloud computing platform called EC2. Users are able to launch virtual machines and computing resources with felxible and cost-efective terms. Tor network can always benefit from more volunteered bandwidth.Using Tor Cloud, anyone can run a Tor bridge or relay and help thousands around the world communicate securely and improve their privacy on the Internet. learn more

How to Get Started

1. Sign-up for an AWS account and subscribe to EC2 service: Sign Up Now!
Amazon will send you an email once your account is activated.

2. Click and start your Tor Cloud bridge on one of the many locations available:

us-east-1
Virginia us-west-1
N. California ap-northeast-1
Tokyo eu-west-1
Ireland ap-southeast-1
Singapore
